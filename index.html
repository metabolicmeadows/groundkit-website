Everything You Need

Your Garden, Intelligently Guided

From soil test to harvest, GroundKit gives you the soil intelligence and planning tools to grow with confidence every season.

NPK Soil Calculator

NPK stands for nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium — the three macronutrients every plant needs. Enter your soil test values and get precise amendment recommendations tailored to your crops and garden beds. Know exactly what your soil needs before you plant.

Precision amendments

Seed Planner

Discover crops matched to your USDA zone, soil, and season. Pin the ones you're growing to track them through the season.

Zone-matched picks

Garden Planner

Lay out your beds, assign crops, and visualize your full growing space. See companion plant suggestions and what was grown in each cell last season — all before you dig a single hole.

Visual bed layout

Planting Calendar

Track planting events on a visual calendar with frost-date awareness. Set your last and first frost dates to see your safe growing window highlighted in green.

Zone-aware scheduling

Amendments & Compost

Browse organic and conventional amendments with an organic-only filter, then plan compost application bed-by-bed with cubic-foot volumes and NPK delivery estimates. Supports standard compost, worm castings, or custom blends.

Organic + bed volumes

Harvest Journal

Log what you grow, when you harvest, and what worked. Add photos, tag events, and link entries to your beds and crops.

Simple Process

From Soil Test to Harvest in 3 Steps

No agronomist required. GroundKit makes precision soil management and garden planning accessible to every grower.

Enter your ZIP code to automatically detect your USDA hardiness zone.
1

Enter Your Soil Results

Input your N, P, and K values from a soil test kit, or use the symptom guide if you don't have a test yet. GroundKit remembers everything across all your beds.

2

Get Amendment Recommendations

Receive precise NPK amendment recommendations for your specific crops and beds — with organic and conventional options you can filter to your preference.

3

Plan, Plant & Grow

Use the seed planner, garden layout tool, and planting calendar together. Your growing season, fully organized and built around your soil and zone.

Local Extension Support

GroundKit connects you to your local Cooperative Extension agent for region-specific advice. Get expert guidance tailored to your county and climate.
